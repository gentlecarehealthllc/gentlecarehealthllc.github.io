Who We Are

Gentlecare Home Health LLC is a trusted home health care agency based in Canal Winchester, Ohio — registered with the Bureau of Labor Statistics and committed to delivering the highest standard of compassionate in-home care.

Our mission is to provide clients and their families with the professional support they need, in the comfort and familiarity of their own homes. Every person deserves to age and heal with dignity.

🌿 Compassionate ❤ Family-First ✔ Certified 🏠 In-Home

Our Promise to You

We treat every client as family. Our Home Health Aides (HHAs) are carefully selected, trained, and supervised to ensure your loved ones receive respectful, reliable, and professional care — every single day.

"Your comfort. Our calling."

Our Key Services

A full range of in-home care services, tailored to meet each client's unique needs.

Personal Care

Bathing, dressing, grooming, and hygiene assistance delivered with warmth and respect for privacy and dignity.

Meal Preparation

Nutritious, home-cooked meals planned and prepared according to dietary needs and personal preferences.

Mobility Support

Safe assistance with moving around the home, transferring from bed to wheelchair, and fall prevention.

Home Assistance

Light housekeeping, laundry, errands, and daily tasks to keep the home clean, safe, and organized.

Toileting Assistance

Respectful and professional help with toileting routines, incontinence care, and related hygiene needs.

Companionship & Daily Activities

Emotional support, conversation, light exercise, and engaging activities to promote mental and social well-being.
